| Objective:   |

Solve the maze.

||||||||||||||||
| How †o Play: |
||||||||||||||||

As the game is running:

Press 'p' : to traverse the maze manually with 'up' & 'down' & 'right' & 'left' arrow keys
Press 'd' : to do depth first search
Press 'b' : to do breadth first search
Press 'r' : to generate a new random maze to solve
Press 'c' : to clear and restart the current maze
